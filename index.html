The BLISTT Circuit

Six Towns. One Thread.

Woven together by ancient trails and highland roads, the BLISTT municipalities offer a circuit of experiences found nowhere else on earth.

SUMMER CAPITAL OF THE PHILIPPINES

Baguio

The summer capital of the Philippines, nestled among towering pine trees. Experience the bustling night market, historic mansions, and the scent of pine in the highland breeze.

Mines View Park Igorot Stone Kingdom Burnham Park

La Trinidad

A vibrant valley where strawberry fields paint the landscape red. Home to the weaving communities and the iconic Valley of Colors mural walk.

Itogon

Where the first rays of highland sun break through mountain peaks. Ancient gold mining trails lead to breathtaking vistas and untouched waterfalls.

Sablan

Rolling hillsides covered in orchards and vegetable terraces. The breadbasket of Benguet where every meal comes from the soil beneath your feet.

Tuba

The gateway municipality where fog blankets winding mountain roads. Pass through centuries-old pine forests on the legendary Kennon Road.

Tublay

Deep in the highlands where Ibaloi traditions remain unbroken. Stone-walled terraces and ancestral homes tell stories spanning generations.

Taste of the Highlands

Food That Carries
Centuries of Memory

Every dish in Benguet is a living archive — smoked, fermented, foraged, and harvested from the same highlands for thousands of years.

Igorot Heritage Dish

Pinikpikan

The Ancestral Chicken

A sacred dish originating from the Igorot tribes of the Cordillera, prepared through a time-honored ritual passed down across generations. The slow-fire cooking draws out a deep, smoky broth unlike any other. More than a meal — it is ceremony.

  • Cooked over open wood fire
  • Served with highland vegetables
  • Deeply ceremonial in origin

Etag

The Smoked Heritage Meat

Traditional slow-cured pork from the Cordillera highlands. Salted slabs of pork are smoked over burning alnos wood. The longer the aging, the deeper the flavor.

  • Smoked over alnos hardwood
  • Aged for weeks to months

Highland Strawberries

La Trinidad's Sweetest Gift

Grown in the cool, fog-kissed soil of La Trinidad Valley. Benguet strawberries are celebrated across the Philippines for their exceptional sweetness and tartness.

  • Best season: November to February
  • Pick-your-own at La Trinidad Farm
